It's a small thing to help one animal, but to that one animal it's a big thing. Adopt your furry friend today! #ProudToBeAPetAdopter

A dog is a dog - pedigree or stray! They all love you unconditionally and selflessly and look beautiful no matter where they come from!
Sadly, people are willing to pay thousands to have a pedigree dog, instead of simply adopting a stray. Adopting a stray is a win-win! They are free, and well accustomed and highly adaptable to the Indian conditions and are naturally very healthy and rarely fall sick.
A dog is a dog no matter what the background! Be smart, adopt!
We are a non-profit organisation based out of Kolkata. Our main target is to help as many street dogs as possible to get a home.
We want to make a difference in the lives of the furry babies on the road, by educating us humans, about how amazing a gift they are to us. Watch them wag, love, kiss, lick, hug, cuddle, protect and comfort you at all times.
How we select: We collect information from residents across the city about new-born street puppies without a mother. We rescue them and keep them with us, feed them to make them healthy and then put them up on our website for adoption.
We also provide vaccinations (including a free anti-rabies injection) and a lifetime of medical check-ups for pets adopted from us.
You can also help us by reporting to us about any such dog in your neighbourhood.
